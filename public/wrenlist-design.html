The problem with other tools

Vendoo and Crosslist are built for listing speed. They're great at taking something you've already decided to sell and pushing it to multiple platforms. That's useful.

But most UK thrifters don't have a listing problem. They have a business clarity problem. What did I pay for this? Where did I source it? Which categories actually make money? Which platforms convert fastest? Which hauls were worth doing again?

Those questions live in a Notes app, a spreadsheet, or nowhere at all. Wrenlist is the answer to all of them — built specifically for the way UK thrifters actually work.

What makes us different

1
Starts at the rack, not the listing form
Log a find the moment you pick it up. Cost, source, condition, photos — before it even makes it home.
2
UK platforms, UK pricing, UK sellers
Vinted, eBay UK, Etsy and Shopify — natively. Pricing in pounds. Built from day one for the UK market.
3
Margin intelligence, not just listing counts
Know your real profit on every item. Which source, which category, which platform makes you the most money.
4
No add-on chaos
AI listings, auto-delist, full platform access — included in the plan. No surprise fees when you actually need the tool to work.

our story
"I kept building spreadsheets to run my reselling business.
They kept breaking.
So I built something that wouldn't."
Dom Cushnan — founder, Wrenlist

By day, I build systems.
By weekend, I source.

I work in technology — specifically the kind where you spend a lot of time thinking about how complex things fit together, where the gaps are, and why well-intentioned processes quietly break. It's work that requires a certain kind of patience with imperfect systems.

On weekends, I switch off. Or try to. I source vintage on the Welsh border — a genuinely good patch for house clearances, small-town charity shops and local auctions that haven't been picked over. I've been doing it for years, first as a hobby, then properly under cushyvintage, now as a business that funds itself and gives me the kind of Saturday I need after a week of screens.

🧥
A Sunday haul from a Welsh border house clearance, March 2025

"I wasn't after listing speed. I wanted to know which car boot was worth the 6am start, and which category was actually making me money."

The tools that existed
weren't built for me.

I tried spreadsheets. Brittle, slow to update at the rack, impossible to get useful numbers out of. I tried Vendoo and Crosslist — solid tools for listing speed, but useless for the questions I actually cared about. What's my real margin on this jacket? Which source gives me the best return? Which platforms are selling, and which are just sitting?

Everything was either too basic, too US-focused, or built for someone who treats reselling as a side hobby rather than a real small business. Nothing started at the rack. Nothing thought in pounds. Nothing tracked the full journey from find to sale.

One evening I started building the thing I needed. Not as a product — as a tool that solved my specific problem. When I mentioned it in reselling communities, the response was immediate. Same frustrations. Same gap.

£0
outside funding.
Built on what it earns.
1
person who uses it
every single weekend
UK
first, always.
Not an afterthought.

What Wrenlist actually is.

It's the tool I use to run cushyvintage. Every feature was built because I needed it. The add-find screen is fast because I use it at the rack, standing in a draughty house clearance at 9am. The margin tracking is prominent because it's the only number that actually matters. The source analytics exist because I wanted to know whether that two-hour drive to Builth Wells was worth it.

Every pricing decision I've made has been as someone who knows what £14 a month means to a reseller still figuring out if this is a hobby or a business. It's not a lot. But it has to earn its keep.

Wrenlist is bootstrapped, independent, and built to stay that way. No VC money means no pressure to bloat it with features nobody asked for, no pressure to optimise for metrics that aren't yours.

getting started
What is Wrenlist?+
Wrenlist is the operating system for UK thrifters and resellers. It lets you log finds at the rack, track your inventory, price items with AI-powered suggestions, crosslist to Vinted, eBay UK, Etsy and Shopify, and see your margins and analytics — all in one place.
Is Wrenlist only for UK sellers?+
Wrenlist is built specifically for UK resellers — currency is GBP, platforms are UK-focused (Vinted UK, eBay UK), and tax/compliance features are aligned to HMRC. Sellers based elsewhere are welcome but some features may not apply to your market.
How do I get started?+
Create a free account, complete the quick onboarding (connecting at least one marketplace takes about 3 minutes), then start logging finds. Your first 10 finds are free — no card required. Start here →
Do I need to connect a marketplace to use Wrenlist?+
No. You can use Wrenlist as an inventory tracker and pricing tool without connecting any marketplace. Crosslisting and auto-delist features require a connected platform, but the core find logging, costing, and analytics work independently.
billing & plans
What does the free plan include?+
The free plan lets you log up to 10 finds per month, connect 1 marketplace, and use the inventory tracker and basic analytics. Crosslisting, AI listing generation, and price suggestions require a paid plan.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings — no questions, no penalties. Your data remains accessible in read-only mode for 30 days after cancellation so you can export anything you need.
How does annual billing work?+
Annual plans are billed once per year and work out to approximately 2 months free compared to monthly billing. You can switch between monthly and annual from your billing settings. Switching to annual mid-cycle prorates the remainder of your current month.
Do you offer refunds?+
We offer a full refund within 14 days of any charge if you haven't used the paid features. After 14 days, or if you've used crosslisting or AI features, we're unable to offer refunds. Contact billing@wrenlist.com with any billing questions.
platforms & crosslisting
Which platforms does Wrenlist support?+
Vinted, eBay UK, and Shopify are fully supported. Etsy is on the roadmap — we're awaiting API approval. We'll notify waitlisted users as soon as Etsy goes live. Vote on the roadmap →
Does auto-delist work on all platforms?+
Auto-delist works on Vinted and eBay UK. When a sale is detected on either platform, Wrenlist automatically removes the listing from your other connected platforms within a few minutes. Shopify inventory sync is also supported. Nester plan and above required.
Will I get flagged by Vinted for using Wrenlist?+
Wrenlist uses Vinted's official developer API within their usage guidelines. We do not scrape, automate logins, or do anything that violates their Terms of Service. However, as with any third-party tool, there is always a degree of platform risk — we recommend reviewing Vinted's current Terms to understand their acceptable use policy.
inventory & finds
Can I bulk import my existing inventory?+
Yes. You can import from a CSV file, or import existing listings directly from a connected platform. We provide a CSV template in the import tool. Go to import →
How does the AI listing generator work?+
On the Forager plan and above, Wren AI generates listing titles and descriptions based on your item details and photos. It's trained on UK resale contexts and uses platform-specific tone — punchy for Vinted, detailed for eBay. You can edit anything it produces before publishing. Available on the Forager plan and above.
What happens to my data if I cancel?+
Your inventory data is retained for 30 days after cancellation in read-only format. You can export your full inventory as a CSV at any time from Settings → Export. After 30 days, data is deleted in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
