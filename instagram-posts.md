## Usage Notes

- **Post on a schedule**: Mon/Wed/Fri at 9am (or adjust to peak engagement time for your audience)
- **Rotate between topics**: Problem → Story → Solution (keeps feed varied)
- **Repost in Stories**: Share each post to Stories 2-3 times in the week it runs
- **Engagement**: Reply to comments within first hour; pin top comments
- **Link to bio**: Rotate between: waitlist signup, pricing page, /story page
- **Update quarterly**: Refresh posts after major feature launches or milestones
