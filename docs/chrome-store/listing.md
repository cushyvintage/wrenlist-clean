# Chrome Web Store Listing — Wrenlist

## Store Name
Wrenlist — Marketplace Sync

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Import finds from Vinted, eBay, Depop & more. Crosslist to 5 platforms. Auto-delist on sale. The reseller's operating system.

## Detailed Description

Wrenlist is the all-in-one inventory tool for vintage resellers, thrifters, and secondhand sellers.

The Wrenlist extension connects your browser to your Wrenlist account so you can:

IMPORT FROM ANY MARKETPLACE
Browse Vinted, eBay, Etsy, Depop, Poshmark, Mercari, Grailed, Facebook Marketplace, Whatnot, or Shopify. Click the import button on any product page — photos, price, title, and description land in Wrenlist instantly.

CROSSLIST TO 5 PLATFORMS
Publish your finds to Vinted, eBay, Etsy, Depop, and Shopify from one screen. The extension fills each platform's listing form automatically — no copy-pasting between tabs.

AUTO-DELIST WHEN YOU SELL
Sold on Vinted? The extension takes down your eBay and Etsy listings automatically. No stale inventory, no double-selling.

PUBLISH QUEUE
Queue items for background publishing while you keep sourcing. List 20 items across 5 platforms without switching tabs.

AI-POWERED LISTINGS
Wren AI drafts titles and descriptions from your photos. Background removal included on paid plans.

TRACK EVERYTHING
Full cost and margin tracking, sourcing trip logs, revenue analytics, tax-ready exports, and price research with live comp data from UK marketplaces.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Install the extension and sign in with your Wrenlist account
2. Browse any supported marketplace — the import button appears on product pages
3. Imported finds appear in your Wrenlist inventory ready to list, price, and publish

SUPPORTED MARKETPLACES
Import from: Vinted, eBay, Etsy, Depop, Poshmark, Mercari, Grailed, Facebook Marketplace, Whatnot, Shopify
Publish to: Vinted, eBay, Etsy, Depop, Shopify

PRICING
The extension is free with any Wrenlist account. Plans start free — upgrade for crosslisting, AI tools, and more.

PRIVACY
Wrenlist only accesses marketplace product pages you visit. We don't track your browsing, sell your data, or read pages outside supported marketplaces. See our full privacy policy at wrenlist.com/privacy.

SUPPORT
Questions? Email admin@wrenlist.com or visit wrenlist.com.

## Notes for Screenshot Capture

To capture store-quality screenshots:

2. Load unpacked in Chrome (chrome://extensions)
3. Use Chrome DevTools device toolbar at 1280x800
4. Navigate to each marketplace for in-context shots
5. Use screenshot tool (Cmd+Shift+P → "Capture full size screenshot")
